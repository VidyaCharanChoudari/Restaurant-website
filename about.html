Our Story

The Urban Spoon was born from a simple idea: create a space where friends gather and every meal feels like a celebration. Since 2020, we've focused on seasonal, locally sourced ingredients to craft flavorful, comforting brunch dishes.

Chef Walter White

Founder & Head Chef — Trained in contemporary culinary arts with a love for farm-fresh flavors and sustainable sourcing.

Values & Commitments

  • Quality ingredients — sourced locally.
  • Sustainability — compostable packaging and waste reduction program.
  • Community — monthly charity brunch events.
